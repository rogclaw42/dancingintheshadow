I am loved. I am thankful. Praying for a strong community to surround all of you. When your pack is strong, you too are strong.

YOUR FRIENDLY HG PUBLIC SERVICE ANNOUNCEMENT:

NEVER say to someone you know has HG:

'Well haven't you tried ginger yet? That worked for my morning sickness.'

In the HG community we call this getting 'GINGERED!'

This is perfectly fine to ask normal newly pregnant moms, but sadly us HG mamas, we are asked this ALL the time, no matter if we're 12 weeks or full-term, as if we haven't already tried that already :)
