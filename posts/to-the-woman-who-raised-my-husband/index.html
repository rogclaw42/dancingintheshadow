To the woman who raised my husband.

I told you at our wedding that I knew he had become the type of man I was honored to marry because he was loved by a mom like you.

As a mother now, I understand how true those words really are. If I could do half the job you did with my sons.

I picture that day, them marrying their loves, me proud of the men I had raised.
